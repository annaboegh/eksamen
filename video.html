A Conceptual Commercial

Somniferum cigarettes - or without the latin name for the flower Opium Cigarettes. I made this concept in order to create a stylised commercial exploring art nouveau and noir aesthetics.
This commercial makes use of the way the different clips are cut, of light, of B-roll clips that sets the scene and how the the woman (in this case, me) smoking the cigarettes slowly becomes affected by the opium - but doing so in a subtle and artistic way. Taking into account that commercials have a selling goal and therefore needs to make something look cool or nice - to make it something others want.
